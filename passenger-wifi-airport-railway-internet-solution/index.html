Delivering High-Speed Internet to Multiple Users −
  • Hotels must support multiple devices per guest, often leading to slow speeds.
  • Advanced bandwidth management ensures fair distribution of internet speed.
  • Tiered user plans allow premium customers to access higher speeds.
  • Firewall-based bandwidth capping prevents excessive data consumption.
Monetization & Revenue Generation from Wi-Fi +
  • Businesses need revenue models to sustain free and paid hotspot networks.
  • Ad-based monetization with video and banner ads on captive portals.
  • Flexible prepaid, postpaid, and voucher-based Wi-Fi plans.
  • Location-based pricing and data limits for different user categories.
Centralized Hotspot Network Management +
  • Managing multiple hotspots from different locations can be challenging.
  • Cloud-based hotspot control panel for centralized network monitoring.
