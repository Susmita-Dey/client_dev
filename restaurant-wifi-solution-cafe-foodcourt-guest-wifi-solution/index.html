Ensuring High-Speed & Uninterrupted Guest Wi-Fi −
  • Cafés and restaurants experience peak-hour congestion with multiple connected devices per table.
  • Fair bandwidth allocation and QoS-based traffic shaping prevent slowdowns and ensure smooth service.
  • Seamless roaming for indoor, outdoor, and drive-thru areas enhances guest experience.
  • Multi-ISP failover and load balancing prevent network outages during high traffic periods.
Secure & Controlled Internet Access for Guests +
  • Public Wi-Fi must be secure and compliant to prevent cyber threats and data breaches.
  • Social media login, SMS OTP authentication, and QR-based guest access ensure safe onboarding.
  • Content filtering and firewall security block harmful websites and unauthorized access.
  • Role-based access control ensures that staff, guests, and management have separate internet privileges
Centralized Multi-Location Network Control & Analytics +
  • Chain restaurants and cafés require centralized control over multiple outlets.
  • Cloud-based monitoring dashboard provides real-time insights into Wi-Fi usage.
  • Location-based analytics and footfall tracking help improve business strategies and guest experiences.
  • Multi-level admin control (ACL) with role-based permissions ensures secure operations across locations.
